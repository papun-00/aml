## Product Overview

Giant tiger prawns sourced from Bay of Bengal wild catch and contracted farms. Prized for bold flavour and firm texture.

Black Tiger Prawns (Penaeus monodon) from Alashore Marine are sourced from both wild-catch Bay of Bengal vessels and contracted aquaculture farms operating under MPEDA supervision. Wild-catch product is processed within 4 hours of landing at our Balasore facility. Sashimi-grade product is available for the Japanese market, meeting Japan's strict 0.01 ppm antibiotic limits. All product is blast-frozen at -40°C and stored at -18°C.

## Product Overview

Traditional sun-dried shrimp with no preservatives. Low moisture content. 12-month ambient shelf life.

Sun-dried shrimp is a traditional Bay of Bengal product with strong demand in Southeast Asian, African, and Middle Eastern markets. Alashore Marine sources from contracted Bay of Bengal catch, dries under controlled sun-drying conditions with moisture content verified at less than 22% before packing. No chemical preservatives or added salt. Available in whole, crushed, and powder formats. Custom retail packing with private label is available. Product is FSSAI and MPEDA compliant.

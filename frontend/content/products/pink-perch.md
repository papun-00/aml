## Product Overview

Mild, white-fleshed Bay of Bengal fish processed within 4 hours of catch. Whole and fillet formats.

Pink Perch, also known as Threadfin Bream (Nemipterus japonicus), is a mild, white-fleshed fish caught in the Bay of Bengal. Alashore Marine processes product within 4 hours of landing. The species is widely used for surimi production in Asia and as a white fish fillet for European and Middle Eastern markets. Our surimi-grade product meets Japanese industrial specifications for colour, gel strength, and protein content. Retail-grade fillet is available individually vacuum-sealed.
